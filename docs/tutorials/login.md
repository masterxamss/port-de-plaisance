<p>The purpose of this tutorial is to show you how to log in to the application.</p>

<p>Follow the steps indicated</p>

<br>

<strong>1 - Access URL  <a href="http://localhost:3000">http://localhost:3000</a></strong> 

![url](/docs/tutorials/images/sign-in/url.webp)  

<br>

<strong>2 - Click on the sign-in button you will then be redirected to the login form </strong>

![sign-in](/docs/tutorials/images/sign-in/home.webp)

<br>

<strong>3 - Fill in the form with the required data and click on sign-in</strong>

![login](/docs/tutorials/images/sign-in/login.webp)

<br>

<strong>4 - After successful login you will be redirected to the application dashboard</strong> 

![alt text](/docs/tutorials/images/sign-in/dashboard.webp)
